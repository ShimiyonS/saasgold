Privacy Policy

SaaSGOLD is committed to protecting your personal and business data. We respect your privacy and take all necessary steps to ensure your information is safe and secure.

1. Information We Collect

  • Business name and contact details
  • Customer details entered by you for chit management
  • Transactional records related to chits and payments

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and manage your chit operations through the SaaSGOLD platform
  • To send notifications, reminders, and reports to your customers
  • To improve our features and personalize your experience

3. Data Privacy & Deletion Policy

Your business and customer data is securely stored and encrypted. If you discontinue the service, we will retain your data for up to 30 days from the termination date.

After 30 days, all your data will be permanently deleted from our servers and cannot be recovered.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties unless required by law.

Refund Policy

SaaSGOLD offers a 30-day refund policy from the date of purchase.

Refund requests will be reviewed and approved based on:

  • Proof of unresolved technical problem
  • Usage of platform beyond acceptable free demo/trial limits

We use Razorpay and PhonePe as our trusted payment gateways to securely process all transactions. Please note that all payments are collected directly through these platforms.

For any queries related to payments, refunds, or cancellations, our support team will handle all requests directly. Customers are encouraged to contact us for assistance, and we are committed to resolving all concerns promptly and transparently.
